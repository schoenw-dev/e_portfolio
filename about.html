About Will

Born and raised in suburban South-East Michigan. Had to learn how to make our own fun away from the cities. Sports and exploring the wilderness aside, the bulk of my childhood centered around the convergence of art and technology. Blockbuster films and video games owned my after-school hours and later my coursework at Central Michigan University.

Contact me at wschoenle111@gmail.com(fake), or use the links to my github and linkedin at the bottom of this page.
